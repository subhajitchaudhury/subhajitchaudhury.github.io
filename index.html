About me

Hi there! I am Subhajit and I am currently working as a Senior Research Scientist at IBM TJ Watson Research Center, New York. My research interests involve improving factuality in generative language models, text-based reinforcement learning and robust computer vision algorithms. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the University of Tokyo, where my dissertation focused on the topic of robust deep learning methods against adversarial vulnerabilities. I received my master's degree in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay and bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University.

Please feel free to email me if you are interested in my work and/or want to collaborate with me.

Work Experience

  • IBM TJ Watson Research Center (Oct 2021 - Present)
    IBM Research - Tokyo (Apr 2017 - Oct 2021)
    Position: Senior Research Scientist
    I am part of the Foundations of AI group. My research interests include (1) Factuality in generative language modeling, (2) Text-based reinforcement learning, and (3) Neuro-symbolic methods for AI transparency. Previously, I worked on robust computer vision applications for real-world applications. My work on robust activity detection was used by Japan Institute of Sports Sciences (JISS) to train the Japanese national team for Tokyo Olympics.

  • NEC Research Laboratories, Japan (Oct 2014 - Mar 2017)
    Position: Researcher
    At NEC, I worked on deep learning based crack detection from videos. Our crack detection system was used for inspections of road cracks in Texas, USA. Additionally, our system was used to find defects on the runaway in the Nanki-Shirahama airport in Wakayama, Japan.
